What We Do

Our business is divided into 3 core practices where we innovate, incubate and build digital solutions that add value to human lives


Digital Innovations

In our Digital Innovations practice, we brainstorm and come up with ideas that solve common challenges through innovative digital solutions. We typically also get pitched to by aspiring entrepreneurs on business ideas they'd like our support.

We build the business models, produce financials and create go-to-market strategies that support creating an MVP (Minimum Viable Product). Depending on our assessment such innovations graduate to our Digital Incubator programme.

LEARN MORE

Digital Incubator

Within our Digital Incubation operations, we look into building digital products and services that we have been vetted and assessed to be viable and commercially sound.

We do sometimes invest our own time and funds in building products and services we're passionate about and are a good product-market fit. We also work with growth partners and investors in some projects that require such synergies.

LEARN MORE

IT Consultancy

In our IT Consultncy practice, we typically work with organisations of all types (public or private) and sizes (small to large) in providing cutting edge IT solutions that could help them transform their businesses and operations digitally.

We're keen players in being part of the nationwide push of transforming the Ethiopian economy digitally by 2025. We look forward to a digitally-empowered Ethiopia soon.
