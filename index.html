About Us

At Fidel Labs, we're a team of technology enthusiast returnees from US, UK and Europe after long stints in building, managing and marketing technology for Fortune 500 Companies. We live and breathe digital and have a focused mission of impacting Ethiopian lives through digtising their daily interactions. Fidel Labs was founded in Addis Ababa in 2020 primarily looking to solve inefficiency gaps leveraging digital technologies. We innovate, experiment and build digital solutions to that end.

What We Do

We work in 3 core areas of practice

Learn More

Digital Innovations

Under our Digital Innovations practice, we brainstorm and come up with ideas that solve common challenges through innovative digital solutions.

We build the business models, produce financials and create go-to-market strategies that support creating a Minimum Viable Product. Depending on our assessment such innovations graduate to our Digital Incubator programme.

Learn More

Digital Incubator

Within our Digital Incubator practice, we look into building digital products and services that we have vetted and assessed will be viable and commercially sound.

We do sometimes invest our own time and funds in building products and services we're passionate about and are a good product-market fit. We do work with growth partners and investors in some projects that require such synergies.

Learn More

IT Consultancy

In our IT Consultncy practice, we typically work with organisations of all types (public or private) and sizes (small to large) in providing cuttring edge IT solutions that could help them transform their businesses and operations digitally. 

We're keen players in being part of the nationwide push of transforming the Ethiopian economy digitally by 2025. We look forward to a digitally-empowered Ethiopia soon.
