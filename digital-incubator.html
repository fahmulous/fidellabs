Digital Incubator

We Breathe Life into Ideas

Our Digital Incubator programme is where we develop an idea or concept into a Minimum Viable Product through a thorough process of business validation steps. Almost all the products or services that come to our Digital Incubator programme go through rigourous business validation processes of buiding business model canvases, validated financials and produced go-to-market strategies that support in building an MVP.

We often invest our time and funds in some projects we're passionate about where we see a good product-market fit. We've also worked with growth partners and investors where we find such synergies would provide better outcomes. 

Some of the projects we have worked on or are working on are:

- MedhanET: is an electronic prescription issuing and management platform that helps patients locate and buy their life-saving medicines and have them delivered to their homes. MedhanET has also been selected as one of 12 innovation challenge grant winners organised by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology in partnership with UNDP in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

- Online Learning Management System (LMS): at the back of the disruption caused by the COVID-19 pandemic on the learning process - we embarked upon building a scalable LMS leveraging open source technology operated on a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model that can be used by Higher Education Institutions.

- Addis Health Care : this is one of our most recent projects that we're excited about which we believe will play a crucial role in the preventative health care space. Stay tuned for details...

- Payment Systems: another new venture we're keen on...details coming soon.
